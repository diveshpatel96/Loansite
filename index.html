How does Zelle® work?

When you enroll with Zelle® through the Bank of Clarkson’s Online Banking or BOC Go Mobile app, your name, the name of your bank and the email address or U.S. mobile number you enrolled is shared with Zelle® (no sensitive account details are shared – those stay with Bank of Clarkson). Bank of Clarkson then directs the payment into your bank account, all while keeping your sensitive account details private.

You can send, request, or receive money with Zelle®. To get started, log in to the U.S. Bank Mobile App or online banking and navigate to Send money with Zelle®. To request money using Zelle®, choose Request money, select the individual from whom you’d like to request money, enter the amount you’d like to request, include and options note, review and hit Select. To receive money, just share your enrolled email address or U.S. mobile phone number with a friend and ask them to send you money with Zelle®.

When you see the purple “Z” next to a contact, you can be confident they’ll receive your payment in minutes. Don't worry – you can still send money to someone who's not enrolled.
